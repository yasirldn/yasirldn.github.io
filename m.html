My name is Muhammad Yasir and I’m an International student interested in improving the well-being of Students which is why I’m running for the position of UWSU VP welfare.

- By not holding a role at SU before, I believe that I can bring positive change by critically analyzing the current issues
- I have been a course representative.

My top 5 objectives if elected as VP welfare are:

1. Helping vulnerable students with cost of living and housing crisis. This includes financial aid, scholarships, and more jobs in the university

2. Introducing free coffee/drinks in study spaces

3. Option of being able to attend lectures online

4. Supporting mental, physical, and emotional well being of students through events and free weekly Pet therapy sessions

5. Reducing Student Advice and Registry response time to a minimum while ensuring same-day appointments and resolutions

The following are further objectives I want to fulfill:
• Promoting students’ spiritual well-being, improving and maintaining prayer spaces, and introducing bidets
• Enhancing students’ employability prospects through technical interviews and organizing on-campus job fairs.
• Providing a sense of belonging to students through more socializing events including play for fun sessions
• Fighting against any form of discrimination and harassment by creating an inclusive environment on campus.
• Keeping students informed and addressing concerns through regular Q&A and update sessions as VP
